Pong-Game!

Chose game type:

1 vs 1.

The rules are simple. This gamemode lets you play against your friend.

Before starting you need to agree on who plays as player 1 and who plays as player 2. Player 1 controls their object with "W" and "S", and player 2 controls their object with "K" and "M".

First one to 10 points win. Have fun!

Practice.

This gamemode is more simple.

No points, no 1 vs 1. Just you against a wall. This gamemode is excellent for when you want to practice to beat your friend woh keeps winning against you in 1 vs 1 mode.

Have fun!
